## Research Profile
B.Eng. graduate from the International College, Mahanakorn University of Technology, with broad interests in LLM safety, trustworthy AI, and rigorous empirical evaluation. My research focuses on practical evaluation methods for language models and AI agents, including failure-case auditing, prompt and instruction robustness, hallucination and risk detection, service-level trust analysis, and statistically reliable model comparison.

My background combines safety-oriented evaluation, service auditing, efficient deep learning, computer vision, and remote-sensing applications. For graduate research, I hope to build evaluation frameworks that make AI system behavior easier to test, compare, and improve.
